About us

Welcome to PRETOUR, where we, the dynamic team of CODERZ, have embarked on a mission to redefine the way you experience the world. As a group of five enthusiastic college students, we passionately present a groundbreaking concept in travel exploration — a Virtual World Tour.


Who We Are

CODERZ is a dedicated team of individuals, each bringing a unique set of skills, creativity, and a shared love for technology. Our journey began with a vision to make global exploration accessible to everyone, transcending the barriers of time and budget constraints.


Our Mission

At PRETOUR, our mission is threefold

1. Offering Virtual Pre-Tours
We understand that planning a trip is a significant decision. To ease your decision-making process, our website provides you with a virtual pre-tour experience. Before you book your tickets for an actual trip, immerse yourself in a digital journey that offers a sneak peek into the wonders awaiting you.
2. Knowledge Enhancement
We believe that every journey is an opportunity to learn and grow. Our platform serves as an educational resource, allowing you to gain valuable insights into the rich cultures, histories, and landscapes of diverse corners of the world. Explore with us, and let the world be your classroom.
